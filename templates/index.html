FAQ Knowledge Base

Working Hours?
Our customer support is available 24/7, 365 days a year.
How to Reset Password?
Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password". A reset link will be sent to your registered email.
Payment Methods?
We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and cash at service centers.
How to Upgrade Plan?
Log into your account portal and go to "Manage Plan" to upgrade, or call our sales line.
Internet Troubleshooting?
Restart your router, check all cables, and ensure your plan is active. Contact support if the issue persists.
Bill Dispute?
Contact billing support or use the Voice Assistant to register a billing complaint. Resolution within 2 business days.
